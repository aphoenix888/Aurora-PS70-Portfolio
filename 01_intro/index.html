<div class="textcontainer"> <br></br> <h3>Week 1: Final Project Proposal</h3> <h4>Idea: Medical School Motivation Box (MSMB) </h4> <p class = "margin"></p> A bit of context: I am going to medical school in the fall. People in medical school are big on using a spaced-repetition flashcard program called Anki to the point of 6 hours/day. Recently, I have also been bad at getting off of my phone at night. I don't feel there is a reason to be on my phone past midnight unless I am away from my dorm; therefore, a designated resting place (aka phone jail) that is time locked would be wonderful. <p class = "margin"></p> An idea for my final project is an Anki clicker and phone holder so after a certain time set by me or before a certain time using my phone is a nuisance (ex. audible alarm, push notifications on my phone). It would be great if there was a tracker for how long my phone is in the box each day and when. I would also want some kind of tracker for task lists so I can maintain an online task list while having a tactile way to mark completion for the task. This will help me focus on which tasks I want to complete each day and hopefully motivate me to do them.
